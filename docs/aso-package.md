## 8. Ratings & Reviews Strategy (post-launch)

- Request in-app review (SKStoreReviewRequest) after the user's **second successful export** — strong positive trigger point
- Prompt: "Loving your highlights? Rate us on the App Store" — appears once per 365 days max (enforced by iOS)
- Respond to all 1-star reviews within 48 hours with a polite acknowledgment and request to email support
- Do NOT incentivize reviews — App Store policy violation
