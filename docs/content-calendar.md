## 1. Content Strategy

### Goal
Drive organic App Store installs via short-form demo content. Target: 10%/month MAU growth from content alone (base case in docs/BUSINESS_CASE.md).

### Platforms (priority order)
1. **TikTok** — widest reach, creator-tool content performs well, algorithm favors new accounts
2. **Instagram Reels** — reaches the same audience, cross-post from TikTok (no watermark — use separate export)
3. **YouTube Shorts** — slower to compound but permanent; good for search ("how to make a highlight reel")
4. **X (Twitter)** — link drops + community engagement; secondary channel

### Content pillars (4 rotating pillars, 2–3 posts/week)
| Pillar | Theme | Frequency | Hook archetype |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Demo** | "Watch AI make a highlight in real time" — screen-record the app | 2×/week | "I turned my [event] into a viral clip in 30 seconds" |
| **Before/After** | Raw footage vs. finished highlight — same clip, show the transformation | 1×/week | "POV: hours of footage → 60-second highlight (AI did the work)" |
| **Niche use case** | Sports, travel, concerts, weddings, workouts — one niche at a time | 1×/week | "If you [play basketball/travel solo/go to concerts], you need this" |
| **Tips/hacks** | 1-tip-per-post about short-form video strategy — drives follows | 1×/week | "The #1 mistake creators make with highlight reels (and how AI fixed it for me)" |

## 3. Hook Templates

Hooks should be tested and rotated. First 2 seconds determine if the viewer stays.

**Demo hooks:**
- "Watch AI find the best moment in my raw [event] footage (this took 30 seconds)"
- "I let AI edit my highlight reel so I didn't have to — here's what it made"
- "POV: you import 2 hours of footage and get this highlight in under a minute"

**Before/After hooks:**
- "Raw vs. finished — same footage, AI did the edit"
- "[Raw clip plays] → [Highlight plays]. Same video. 30 seconds of work."

**Niche hooks:**
- "If you play [sport/attend concerts/travel solo], you NEED to see this"
- "Every [athlete/traveler/parent] should be using this to capture their memories"

**Tips hooks:**
- "The #1 mistake people make when posting highlight reels"
- "Why your [sport/travel] highlights aren't going viral — and one trick to fix it"
