# HighlightMagic — Vision (the WHY + the taste/quality bar)

Read alongside `ROADMAP.md` (what to build, in what order) and `FACTORY_STANDARD.md` (how the loop
operates). ROADMAP/VISION win on any product-specific. This file is the "why" and the design bar the
loop optimizes for.

## The one-liner
**Anyone becomes a content creator.** Dump in the raw pics + videos from a real-life moment — a July 4th
weekend, a trip, a birthday, a night out — and HighlightMagic turns them into a polished, on-trend,
vertical reel worth posting, with **zero editing skill required**.

## Who it's for
NOT pro editors. The casual person whose camera roll is full of moments they never do anything with —
because editing is tedious and they don't think of themselves as "a creator." We remove that barrier
entirely: **upload → reel.**

## The "aha" (the moment that makes the product)
*"I dropped in my weekend and got back a reel I'm actually proud to post"* — in minutes, with almost no
decisions. **Time-to-first-shareable-reel** is the metric that matters most; every second of friction
before that aha is the enemy.

## The north star (where it goes)
It doesn't stop at an export. The full loop is **moment → posted reel**: the app publishes the finished
reel straight to the user's OWN Instagram / TikTok / YouTube Shorts / Facebook — the "director
connectors" — so going from a pile of clips to a live post is effectively **one tap**.
*(Phase 2 / post-launch — see ROADMAP Track I. Platform APIs gate direct posting to Business/Creator
accounts + app review, so v1 launches via the OS share sheet, which works for everyone.)*

## The bar (what "good" means — the loop optimizes for THIS, not feature count)
- **Output looks intentionally EDITED, on-trend, and share-worthy** — never template slop, never
  obviously AI-generated filler. A real person should want to post it without embarrassment. The design
  taste test: *would a good human editor have chosen these cuts, this pacing, this music sync?*
- **Input is dead-simple:** dump media, make almost no decisions, get a great default. Deeper control is
  optional, never required.
- **The win condition is users actually POSTING and SHARING the reels.** Organic share loops are both the
  growth engine and the truest PMF signal. Build toward *"so good they share it,"* not
  *"feature-complete."*

## What it is NOT
- Not a pro NLE (Premiere/CapCut for experts).
- Not a generic AI-video toy or a template filler.
- The entire value is: **real personal footage in → a genuinely postable reel out, effortlessly.**
